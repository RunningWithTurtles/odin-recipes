Khachapuri

Description

A tradional Georgian dish made of bread, cheese and butter.

There are several variations, some with eggs, more bread of different cheeses

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Bread
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Bake the bread
  2. Add in the butter
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Put back in the over
  5. Add the egg and salt
  6. Feast!
