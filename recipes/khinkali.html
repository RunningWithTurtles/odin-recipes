Khinkali

Description

A doughy ball containing different meats and veggies

Eaten by holding the top portion of the food

Something to remember when eating Khinkali is to suck the juice out on the first bite

(It's the best part)

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Meat(Pork, Beef, Chicken)
  • Broth
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat by mixing the ingredients
  2. Roll the dough flat
  3. Place the meat in the dough and roll it into balls
  4. Put the balls into the oven
  5. Enjoy!
