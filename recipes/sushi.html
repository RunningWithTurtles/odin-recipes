Sushi

Description

A delicious Japanesse dish prepared from raw fish

It includes rice, cream cheese and avacado,

and wrapped in seaweed. It can be served cold or fried

Ingredients

  • Raw Fish
  • Seaweed
  • Rice
  • Avacado
  • Cream Cheese
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Lay the seaweed out
  2. Place the rice flat on the seaweed
  3. Add the fish
  4. Add the Cream cheese
  5. Add the avacado
  6. Roll it up
  7. Dip in soy sauce
